Hello, my name is Stephanie Recla. I was born in the "crown city" of Culver City, Ca in 1985. My mother was my biggest influence in pursuing an artistic lifestyle. She always said that there are no mistakes in art, and I always believed it can change lives.

After serving 3yrs as an active duty member in the Air Force, my quest is to pursue my passion for creating and learning. I am new to the world of graphic design, so my style is still in development, however while I explore and experiment with new mediums you can find me drinking coffee, painting my nails, and loving two cute kitties.
